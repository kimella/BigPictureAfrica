Who We Are

Big Picture Africa is a youth-driven initiative to make impact and create change in the continent of Africa. We have a predilection for neglected but important initiatives and we aim to identify all such areas and create positive change.

As a non-profit organization, our aim is to create and implement projects surrounding the UN 2030 sustainable development goals (SDGs) that will take Africa a step closer to being an enabling environment for the growth and sustenance of the dreams and aspirations of all with an African heritage. We have a special interest in young, vulnerable and disadvantaged Africans across the continent and beyond.
